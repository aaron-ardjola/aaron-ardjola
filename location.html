What to Do

Activities near Durrës

Durrës is on the shores of Adriatic Sea, a short trip away from Tirana. Here's a small sampling of the things you can do in the area.

Eat & Drink

The famous “Mrizi i Zanave Agroturizëm” is only 1.5h away by car. Here you can spend some time eating the locally grown and raised food while enjoying the beautiful view.

Relax

Spend a day at the sea and relax at the beach of Golem or Durrës.

Explore

Drive south through the city of Vlorë and up into the mountains on the Llogara Pass giving you a stunning view of the Ionian Sea over to the island of Corfu.
