Please join Ardjola & Aaron on September 1st, 2024 as we celebrate our wedding in Albania.

Ardjola’s Story

Ardjola grew up in the Fier, Albania, spending much of her childhood dreaming about studying abroad. She studied electrical engineering at the TU Berlin in Germany. Today, she works as PhD student on the topic of lithium-ion batteries.

Aaron’s Story

Aaron was born in Berlin and spent much of his life in Switzerland, returning to Berlin at the age of 18 to study electrical engineering at the TU Berlin. Nowadays, he works as a PhD student on the control of wind turbines.

Our Relationship

We first met in the first semester of our bachelor studies. After multiple years, we started becoming friends in 2018. We were engaged in December 2023, and now we're getting married!
